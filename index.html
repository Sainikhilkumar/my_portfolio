Smart Trolley

The Smart Shopping Trolley is an innovative project aimed at revolutionizing the retail shopping experience by integrating automation and smart technology. It leverages IoT, sensors, and embedded systems to automate product identification and streamline billing processes. The trolley is equipped with RFID or barcode technology to scan items in real time, with a digital interface displaying the total bill and providing alerts for budget limits or product availability. Designed to minimize human error and reduce checkout times.

Automatic Irrigation

The Automatic Irrigation System is a smart solution designed to optimize water usage in agricultural and gardening applications. This project utilizes soil moisture sensors and microcontrollers to monitor real-time soil conditions and automate the irrigation process. By analyzing moisture levels, the system ensures that water is delivered only when necessary, reducing wastage and promoting sustainable water management.
